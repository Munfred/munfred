We have experience

Our two master growers have over 11 years’ experience growing in industrial and artisanal environments, using soil, coco coir, rockwell cubes and love.

We are hands on

We eschew automation in favor of being hands on and entirely manual. We are in the grow rooms every day, looking at the plants and adjusting their water, nutrients, stress levels, lighting and temperature.

WE ARE ORGANIC

We grow organically, avoiding cocoa and other GMO crapin our medium. That's why it's the best medium, for the best organic pot. Organic pot trumps inorganic pot.

The pot economy

“Legal marijuana is the fastest-growing industry in the United States and if the trend toward legalization spreads to all 50 states, marijuana could become larger than the organic food industry” .

THEY STILL REGULATE POT

The Maine Marijuana Legalization Measure legislates a limit of 800,000 square feet of plant canopy for Retail Marijuana Cultivation facilities. That encompasses all flowering, vegetative, cloning, mothering and quarantine square footage.

The pot wave

Seven states and the District of Columbia have adopted the most expansive laws legalizing marijuana for recreational use. Most recently, California, Massachusetts, Maine and Nevada all passed measures in November legalizing recreational marijuana.

Our philosophy

We have neither the interest in nor the intention to automate. Marijuana is not a commodity. You can automate your way into growing good pot, but if you want to grow super-premium marijuana, you need to know the plant, know how to grow it and tend to the individual needs of each crop.
