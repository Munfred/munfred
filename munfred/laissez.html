As your team grows, it gets harder to get the best ideas

Whether it’s because someone missed the lunch conversation, had to skip a meeting,
works across a different group, or simply can’t find out who to talk with,
it’s easy to miss on a valuable contribution.

Laissez makes it easy to create challenges and crowdsource solutions within your team.

Define a challenge

By framing well articulated problems and requirements, challenges can prompt internal crowds to generate high quality and actionable insights, unlocking new solutions and engaging participants.

Invite participants

Challenges can focus on solving a specific problem, or inviting everyone to think broadly and creatively. Laissez was built to facilitate all types of innovation, whether it’s improving existing processes or coming up with completely novel ideas.

Gather submissions

Good ideas can come from anywhere in your team. Laissez makes it easy to collect and evaluate them.

Integrate with Slack

Slack is where workplace communication happens. Laissez let's you seamlessly integrate with Slack so that users can focus on what matters.
